1. Hero

   - Current role or academic status
   - Research identity in one sentence

2. Research Snapshot

3. Selected Work
   - 3 highlighted publications, working papers, or projects
   - Short 1-2 sentence descriptions

4. Teaching and Mentoring
   - Teaching areas
   - Course or TA experience
   - Student-facing strengths

5. Contact Footer
   - Email
   - Institutional affiliation
   - Scholar profiles and code links
