# Launch Checklist

## Content

- Homepage copy is specific and not generic.
- CV PDF opens correctly.
- Publication links work.
- Contact email is correct.
- Profile links work.
- Last updated date is visible on the CV page.

## Design

- Headshot looks professional on desktop and mobile.
- No text overlaps on mobile.
- Buttons and links have clear hover/focus states.
- Font sizes are readable.
- The site looks good in light mode.

## Technical

- HTTPS works.
- `www` redirects correctly.
- `robots.txt` exists.
- Favicon exists.
- Site title and meta description are set.
- Open Graph preview image is set if sharing on social platforms.

## Academic Search Committee Test

Ask one colleague to find the following in under one minute:

- CV
- Research agenda
- Teaching interests
- Contact email
- Job-market paper or strongest featured work
