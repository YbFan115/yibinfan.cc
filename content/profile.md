## Short Bio

Yibin Fan is a PhD student in Communication at the University of Washington. His research sits at the intersection of political communication, computational social science, and online communities, with a focus on how political meaning emerges in spaces that are not always explicitly political.

His dissertation, "Everything to do with Politics: Examining Politicization in Online Communities," studies politicization in digital public communication. His work examines incidental political discussion, incivility, platform migration, local-oriented online communities, and the relationships among digital groups.
